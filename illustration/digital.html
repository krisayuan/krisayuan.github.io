HANAHAKI

2018

Hanahaki is a series on the Japanese urban legend Hanahaki Disease(花吐き病). The disease is brought on by unrequited love and causes a flower to grow in the victim's chest cavity. As their feelings get stronger, the flower grows larger until it eventually kills them.
