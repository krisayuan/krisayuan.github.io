SMILE MEDICINE

illustration + asset production, 2020

Smile Medicine is a single-player, magical realist adventure dealing with loss and trauma developed for mobile and Switch. After developing early-onset Parkinson’s, an avid hiker confronts her fears on her final trek. Following the trail on which her mother disappeared, Nila must accept it will be the closest she can get to the person she needs most.

ASCEND

game design + narrative, 2018-19

ASCEND is an immersive, interactive theater experience based on world mythology that ran at USC and at the 2019 Hollywood Fringe Festival. Gods of four mythologies call upon you, their demigod children, to save them from a rising threat. Go on quests to find the truth, prove yourself in a divine tournament and decide how this story ends. Only you can save the world in this interactive, immersive, multi-room experience.

CHIMERA

illustration + narrative, 2018-19

Chimera is a narrative-focused, turn-based combat game combining research-informed concepts of hope and perseverance in an entertainment game developed for PC. The hope system was designed based on optimism research, especially explanatory style, and what that means for motivation and how we behave in difficult situations. Chimera is the second ever game to come out of the USC Games and Health Program.

TOFU KID

art + narrative, 2017

Tofu Kid is a mobile platformer game that was developed at the CMU Game Academy. It focuses on the prince of the forest who has lost his crown and must jump to unbelievable heights to get it back. On the way he must avoid crows, dragons, and aliens, not to mention his own anxieties about ruling a kingdom. Oh, and he’s also made of tofu.
